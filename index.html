The Hearth Circle
"Where everyone has a seat at the fire"

Welcome to the Hearth Circle—a warm, welcoming community built on kindness, loyalty, and sincerity. Here, every voice matters, and every member is invited to pull a chair up to the fire.

Whether you’re exploring Palia, sharing your journey, or simply looking for genuine connections, this is a place where you belong.

Rules

  • ✅ 18+ only – For a mature, grounded community.
  • ✅ Free speech with respect – Speak your truth, but honor others.
  • ✅ We help because we care – Support flows naturally here.
  • ✅ Be open, honest, and present – Listen deeply, share freely, and grow together.
  • ✅ This isn’t just another community – We’re not here just to farm or trade — we welcome those who bring value through kindness, loyalty, and sincerity.

Join us

To take a seat at the fire, you must be invited by one of our trusted members through recommendation.

Join our discord to play with community members and if we determine that you are a good fit then you will be invited!
